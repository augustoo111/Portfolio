All About Great Ocean


The Great Ocean Road travelling to the seaside city of Warrnambool on a small luxury tour with your friendly local guide departing Geelong. This is a three day/two night tour. The journey begins as your local guide entertains you with local stories and history as you wind your way down the breath taking Great Ocean Road.
Stopping along the way you will discover it’s past and experience it’s today. Sample the seafood at the local fishing towns. As you hug the cliffs check out the surfers taking on the Southern Ocean.

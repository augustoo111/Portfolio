About Me

An enthusiastic IT learner on a journey to turn my passion into a career. Originally from Myanmar, I hold a Bachelor of Law (LL.B) degree, but my fascination with technology and coding led me to change paths and pursue the Diploma of IT at RMIT University in Melbourne, Australia.

My adventure into IT began when I managed and created websites using templates. However, I yearned to move beyond templates and deepen my understanding of web development. The thrill of building interactive and user-friendly websites drove me to explore programming languages like Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I am driven by a desire to master the art of web development, from creating clean and effective code to crafting seamless user experiences. My dedication to learning, problem-solving mindset, and adaptability make me a valuable team member ready to take on new challenges and contribute to innovative projects.

Outside the world of coding, I love traveling to new places, experiencing diverse cultures, and connecting with people. This enriches my perspective and fuels my creativity in the digital realm.

I am a dedicated and proactive team member, feel free to reach out to me. I would love to discuss how my enthusiasm for IT can bring value to your organization.
